Classic snake game

Yes, as you guessed it, this game is classic snake game. Here is a capture of game screen. This is one of the simplest games I have built. Basically, I have used an array of 0s and 1s to represent a snake and ground. The movement of snake can be achived by adding a 1 in a certain direction(i.e along the direction of travel of snake) and removing a 1 from the end(i.e cutting down its tail)
